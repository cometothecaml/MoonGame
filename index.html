How do I play?

There is a button that will show up once you connect Metamask. The button has a timer which can be reset by sending moons to the contract. The price to reset the timer starts at 1 MOON and increases by 0.01 MOON every time the button is clicked. The last person to reset the timer gets all the MOONs in the contract. The timer resets to 100 blocks, or 25 minutes every time the button is clicked. Once the timer reaches zero the last person who reset the timer can cash out the entire balance. There is no founder fee or anything. You will receive everything in the contract.
